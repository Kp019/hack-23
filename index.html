Our Venue

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam, pioneered engineering education in central Kerala in 1961. Managed by the Mar Athanasius College Association and aided by the Government of Kerala, the college was the first in Asia under Christian Management. MACE can offer its huge ethereal campus that can house such a colossal meetup. With its six well-equipped seminar halls, Open air theatre, Main College Auditorium, and Indoor stadium, MACE has the infrastructure to support multiple conferences simultaneously. The serene surroundings and clean air make this a perfect location for educational excellence. Kothamangalam is well connected by road from all parts of Kerala State.
